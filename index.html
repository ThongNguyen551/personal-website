Hi, I'm Thong Nguyen, a Senior Data Scientist with experience spanning industrial computer vision, food inspection, medical imaging, fraud detection, and credit risk modeling. My work focuses on transforming machine learning research into production systems that deliver measurable business impact as well as enhance human life quality.

I have worked at Zalopay (VNG Corporation), where I have developed machine learning solutions for credit scoring, fraud investigation, and risk monitoring. Previously, I worked at Tordivel in Norway, building computer vision systems for industrial inspection, object detection, image segmentation, and automation.

I hold an Erasmus Mundus Master's degree in Computational Colour and Spectral Imaging (COSI), studying across France, Norway, and Finland. My academic background includes computer vision, hyperspectral imaging, machine learning, and medical image analysis.

My interests lie at the intersection of Computer Vision, Multimodal AI, Vision-Language Models, Agentic Systems, Industrial AI, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation. I am particularly interested in building AI systems that bridge cutting-edge research with real-world deployment.
